Goals

Specific Goals


Our overall aim is to develop a rapid, reproducible, and open data re-use model for policy-relevant COVID-19 and pandemic preparedness research in low- and middle-income countries, focusing on vaccine effectiveness and early warnings of local outbreaks that take local social deprivation into account.


Link with Data Governance and Capacity Strengthening Cores

The data re-use project will serve as a use-case for both cores. We will leverage our extensive experience in OHDSI data science tools, standards, and processes for this.

Link with Ongoing Initiatives and Resources

The Brazil team is running a COVID-19 Vaccine Effectiveness Surveillance Platform (https://vigivac.fiocruz.br), and we already have been able to run comparative analysis in cooperation with other countries before.
